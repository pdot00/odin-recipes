Stuffed Eggplant

Stuffed Eggplan Ingredients

  • 1 (1 1/2 pound) eggplant, halved lengthwise
  • 3 tbps. Olive Oil, divided
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/2 pound sweet italian sausage, casings removed
  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • 1 cup sliced fresh mushrooms
  • 4 cloves garlice, minced
  • 2 tbpls. chopped fresh basil
  • 1 cup tomato-basil pasta sauce
  • 3/4 cup italian seasoned panko bread crumbs, divided
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese divided

Steps

  1. Scoop out the flesh, brush the shells with oil, and season
  2. Chop the reserved eggplant flesh into bite-sized pieces
  3. Cook sausage, vegetables, and garlic in the remaining Oil
  4. Stir in the reserved eggplant, basil, and sauce. Mix in some of the pank and Cheese
  5. Stuff the shells and springkle the remaining chees and panko over the top
  6. Bake until the eggplant is tender
